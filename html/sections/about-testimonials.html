Grief is a powerful emotion...

...and unfortunately we are taught to react to the emotional feeling in our hearts by using our minds. Society unknowingly offers us false information as we look to deal with these feelings. We look to our friends and family during these difficult times and unfortunately the responses we are given do nothing to help the hurt that remains present in our hearts.

Can you identify with some of the unhelpful and maybe even hurtful comments made by others?

Time heals all wounds.

If you just take more time to be alone, you will feel better

Don’t feel bad, she/he is in a better place.

You really need to get a new pet and you will be happy again.

Just keep yourself really busy and you won’t have time to think about it.

Why don’t you just get away for a couple of days to get your mind off of it?

Ouch! Those words are painful and confusing. They didn't help or you would be feeling better.
