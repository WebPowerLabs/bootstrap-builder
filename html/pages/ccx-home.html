What is the Cannabis Commodities Exchange?

CCX is a secure, transparent online wholesale trading platform for state‐licensed cannabis businesses to buy, sell, trade, and collect data on cannabis products with ease and convenience.

The Problem

Cannabis cultivators and products manufacturers often fall victim to poor harvests, inconsistent testing results, demand spikes, and regulatory intervention. These pitfalls affect all growers and producers, regardless of expertise, and are constantly limiting the supply of inventory held by dispensaries.

Traditional Forms of Wholesaling Are Inefficient, Expensive and Frustrating.

Limited Network

Small networks give few choices in ability to meet consumer demand.

Limited Choices

Limited networks result in limited wholesale variety, inconsistent quality, and dissatisfied customers.

High Prices

Urgency to meet demand leads to paying top dollar for inferior cannabis products.

Don't Wait Until the Last Minute, Expand Your Network and Meet Demand More Efficiently.

The Solution

Using CCX, members can create purchase requests if they are running low on inventory, browse sell requests to see if another member can fulfill their needs, and list any of their surplus products for sale. CCX also tracks and stores trade data in real time. As a result, CCX members can become savvy buyers and sellers of cannabis products by reviewing the trade data CCX generates, like historical pricing data and demand trends. Let CCX make your business more profitable and your life as a business owner easier.

CCX in a Nutshell

List and Browse

You can create purchase or sell requests or browse requests to see if another member can fulfill your needs.

Inquire and Negotiate

You can inquire about listings posted by other users and negotiate terms on our in‐application messaging system, and receive message notifications via text message.

Trade with Ease

You can easily choose from 10 different product types to create over 100 million possible product listings—all at the click of a button on your computer or swipe of your finger on a mobile device, making your life easy and business more profitable.

Do it all with just one platform

CCX has been built with input and guidance from leading cannabis business owners to best meet the needs of licensed cannabis businesses. The CCX platform includes numerous features and functionalities that businesses need to conduct wholesale trading, plus the best service and support to help make your life easier and business more profitable.

10 Different Product Types

CCX breaks down product types into flower, trim, shake, clone, concentrate, edibles, topical, seeds, tinctures, and pre-rolls so that you can easily be precise.

Multiplatform Use

CCX has been designed with the active businessman in mind. Whether you’re in the office or on the go, you can use CCX by clicking your computer or swiping your finger on your mobile device. CCX’s real-time listing updates make sure you are on top of everything that is happening in your marketplace.

Advanced Database

CCX has an advanced database that collects live trade data every time a transaction occurs on the marketplace. We will compile this data and provide it to our users so they can make informed wholesale decisions. This information will include historical strain prices and seasonal demand trends.

Color Coding and Badges

CCX's intuitive color coding and badging systems make searching and sorting easy by allowing users to quickly decipher information about listings.

Single-Frame Design

Our unique design allows you to conduct all of your trading on a single-page without ever having to reload a new one. This makes using our system very efficient and intuitive.

128 Bit Encryption

Our encryption methodology provides a secure platform for you to conduct trades.

User Friendly

For ease of use, you can sort and filter listings by strain name, dominance, product type, price, licensee etc., and each listing has a badge and expanded view that help you quickly decipher pertinent information about the product and licensee. Moreover, CCX allows its users to take pictures of their product so you know what it looks like and CCX encourages its users to upload their test results as well to ensure the product is free of contaminants.

Distinct Marketplaces

CCX only permits trading between licensees in the same state to ensure compliance, and each state that has both retail and medical licensees has separate and distinct marketplaces for each.

Transport Manifest

Upon completing a transaction, CCX will automatically fill out a transport manifest documenting the details of the trade.

App and Mobile Messages

CCX allows you to conduct trades using our in-app messaging application, which has been designed like an email inbox. If you’re on the go, you can also receive text message alerts.

Affiliate Connections and Membership Discounts

The CCX community offers its members a unique business-to-business network. CCX has partnered with vendors (ex: delivery) to offer its users member benefits. CCX is also partnering with various cannabis industry associations to offer their members discounted services.

Feedback and Review

CCX’s marketplace is regulated by its members. CCX encourages all members to provide feedback and reviews regarding their transaction or experience with other licensees so that each member improves their performance and we, collectively, help professionalize the industry.

2 Factor Authentication

Our authentication methods ensure that only state-licensed marijuana businesses are trading on CCX, so members know they are only dealing with legitimate businesses.

No Transaction Fees
